Ordering & Payment
How do I place an order?›

Browse our collection, add items to your cart, fill in your details at checkout, and transfer the payment via bank transfer. WhatsApp us your payment screenshot to confirm.

What payment methods do you accept?›

We accept bank transfer to HBL. Account details are shown at checkout. All payments are in PKR — no foreign currency conversion needed.

Is bank transfer safe?›

Yes. We use a verified HBL account. Always save your transfer screenshot and share it with us on WhatsApp to confirm your order immediately.

Can I cancel my order?›

Orders can be cancelled before we place them with our US supplier (typically within 24 hours). After that, the advance is non-refundable as the order has been placed.

Why can't I get a Google rate conversion?›

Our PKR prices already include import duties, international shipping, and our service fee — all bundled in. A Google rate comparison doesn't account for these costs, so it's not an accurate comparison.

Shipping & Products
How long does delivery take?›

Preorders take approximately 4 weeks from order confirmation. In-stock items ship within 3–5 business days within Pakistan after payment is confirmed.

Do you deliver to my city?›

Yes! We deliver to all major cities across Pakistan including Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, Multan, Peshawar, and more. WhatsApp us if you're unsure about your area.

Are your products authentic?›

Absolutely. We source every product directly from US retailers — no third parties, no copies. Authenticity is our core promise. If it's not real, we won't sell it.

Do shoes come with the original box?›

Shoes are shipped without the original box by default to keep shipping costs low. If you need the box, WhatsApp us before ordering — there may be an additional charge.

Can I request a specific product?›

Yes! WhatsApp us with the product you're looking for. We'll check availability and pricing. Custom sourcing is available for most US retail brands.

Still have a question?

We're on WhatsApp and reply within 2 hours during business hours.
