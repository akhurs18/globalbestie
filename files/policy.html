Before You Order

Preorder & Delivery Policy

Please read — we want zero surprises on both sides.

50% Advance Required

Pay 50% of the total to confirm your order. Remaining 50% is due before dispatch from our end.

4 Weeks Delivery

Preorder delivery takes approximately 4 weeks from order confirmation. This covers sourcing + international + local courier.

Shoes Without Box

Shoes are shipped without the original box by default to keep shipping costs low. Box available on request — additional charge may apply.

Fixed Prices

All prices are final and fixed. No discounts. Prices include all taxes, customs duties & shipping — no hidden fees at any stage.

No Google Rates

We cannot provide Google conversion price comparisons. Our PKR prices account for import duties and shipping — a direct rate conversion is not accurate.

Third-Party Courier

Courier companies are not under our control. Once dispatched, delivery tracking is via the courier. Dispatch ≠ immediate delivery.

Cancellations

Cancel within 24 hours of order confirmation for a full refund. After that, your advance is non-refundable as the order has been placed with our US supplier.

Exchanges & Returns

Due to the nature of international sourcing, we cannot accept returns or exchanges unless the item is demonstrably not as described. WhatsApp us if you have a concern.

Authenticity Guarantee

Every product is sourced directly from US retailers. If you receive something that is not authentic, contact us immediately — we will make it right.
