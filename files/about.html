Our Story

Born from a love of authentic American style

Sourcing the real thing from the USA, delivered to Pakistan.

Home›About Us

Global Bestie started with one simple frustration: why is it so hard to get real, authentic American products delivered to Pakistan? Not copies. Not knock-offs. The real thing — straight from US shelves to your door.

We personally source every item — from Charlotte Tilbury lipsticks to Coach bags to Nike sneakers — from US retailers. Every product goes through our hands before it reaches yours. That's our promise, and it's why we say no fakes, ever.

Based in Pakistan, built for Pakistan. All prices are in PKR, all communication is on WhatsApp, and we're here every step of the way — from browsing to your doorstep.

We started small — just a handful of products and a lot of passion. Today we carry 200+ curated items across makeup, skincare, bags, fashion, and more. Every single one sourced fresh from the USA.
